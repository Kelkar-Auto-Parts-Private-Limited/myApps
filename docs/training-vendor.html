Vendor Training Guide

Role: Vendor

This guide is for transport vendors who use the KAP VMS to view and track trips assigned to their vehicles. As a vendor, you have read-only access to your trip data.

Table of Contents
1. Logging In 2. Viewing Your Trips 3. Understanding Trip Status 4. Vehicle & Driver Information 5. Filtering & Searching 6. Frequently Asked Questions

1. Logging In

You see the KAP VMS login page with the company logo, username/password fields, a math CAPTCHA, and a database connection indicator.
Step 1: Enter the username and password provided by your KAP administrator.
Step 2: Solve the math CAPTCHA and click Sign In.
Step 3: You are taken directly to the My Trips (Vendor) page.
Your account is linked to your vendor record in the system. You can only see trips assigned to your vendor company. If you have multiple vehicles, all their trips appear together.
Check the "Remember me" box to stay logged in on the same device. If your account is locked after failed attempts, wait 60 seconds and try again.

2. Viewing Your Trips

The "My Trips (Vendor)" page shows your vendor name below the title. At the top is a search bar, followed by date range filters (1D, 1W, 1M, 1Y with From/To date pickers). Below the filters are vendor, vehicle, and status dropdowns. Summary statistics cards show trip counts and totals. The trip list appears as a scrollable table or card layout.
Step 1: The page loads with the current month's trips by default (1M filter active).
Step 2: Each trip entry shows: Trip ID, date, route (from/to locations), vehicle number, vehicle type, trip status, and amount.
Step 3: Click on a trip to view its full details including all segments, timestamps, and photos.

3. Understanding Trip Status

Trips move through the following stages:

StatusMeaning
BookedTrip has been created by KAP. Your vehicle is expected at the pickup location.
In ProgressVehicle has exited the KAP gate. The trip is underway.
CompleteAll segments are done: gate exit, delivery, gate entry, material receipt, and approval are finished.
You cannot modify trip data. If you notice an error (wrong vehicle, wrong date, etc.), contact the KAP Trip Booking user or your administrator.

4. Vehicle & Driver Information

Each trip displays the assigned vehicle and driver details:

  • Vehicle Number — the registration plate of the assigned vehicle
  • Vehicle Type — category (e.g., 9MT, 16MT, 32MT Trailer)
  • Driver Name — the driver on record for the trip
  • Route — origin and destination locations for each segment
If a driver or vehicle needs to be changed for an upcoming trip, inform the KAP office before the trip starts so they can update the booking.

5. Filtering & Searching

Search: Type a Trip ID or vehicle number in the search bar at the top to instantly filter the list.
Date range: Click 1D (today), 1W (this week), 1M (this month), or 1Y (this year). Or pick custom dates using the From/To date pickers.
Vehicle filter: If you have multiple vehicles, use the vehicle dropdown to see trips for a specific vehicle only.
Status filter: Select Booked, In Progress, or Complete to see only trips in that stage.
Summary statistics update in real-time as you apply filters, showing the count and total amount of visible trips.

6. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: I cannot see a trip that was booked today.

Make sure your date filter includes today. Click the "1D" button to show today's trips only. If it still does not appear, the trip may be assigned to a different vendor or not yet created.

Q: The trip amount seems incorrect.

Trip rates are set by KAP administrators in the Trip Rate Master. Contact your KAP coordinator to discuss rate discrepancies.

Q: I forgot my password.

Contact your KAP administrator to reset your password. They can do this from the User Management section in the portal.

Q: Can I update driver or vehicle details?

No. Vendor accounts have view-only access. Request changes through your KAP contact who manages trip bookings.
